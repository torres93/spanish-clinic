Spanish Clinic, LLC

Spanish Clinic foresees that it is important and imminent that culturally appropriate services be in place for a diverse population in need of Clinical Services.

We will provide services primarily but not exclusively in the Spanish language. We will be at the service of individual clients and agencies from the community and the criminal justice system.

We are:

* MEDICAID provider for Substance Abuse and Mental Health.

* Division of Youth Corrections (DYC) provider.

* Department of Corrections (DOC) provider

* Senate Bill 94 provider for the 1st and 18th Judicial Districts.

* Denver Department of Human Services provider.

We offer:

* DUI Classes

* Substance Abuse Services for Youth

* Aggression Control Classes

* Mental Health Services

* On-Site UA’s and BA’s

Spanish Clinic is Division of Behavioral Health licensed for:

* Intensive Outpatient Treatment

* Enhanced Outpatient Treatment

* Outpatient Treatment

* Level I DUI Education

* Level II DUI Education & Therapy

* Offender Education & Treatment

* Services to Child Welfare Clients

* Treatment Program Specific to Minors

* Treatment Program Specific to Women
