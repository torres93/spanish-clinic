Spanish Clinic, LLC

In Home Clinician

Spanish Clinic, LLC is looking for a highly energetic, positive person that is reliable, self-motivated, and willing to be part of a dynamic growing program.

Job Title: In Home Clinician 

Summary: The In Home Clinician is a full time position with activities related to In Home Services coordination and Supervised Visits. The person would work with families with different needs related to cases open with the department of human services. The person will be providing supervised visits to children and their relatives for families involved with the department of human services.

Job Type: Full Time. 

Compensation: Salary employee. Annual from $29,120.00 to $33,280.00 USD based on education and/or experience.

Benefits:

* 6 paid holidays per year: Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day and New Year Day.

* 3 hours of paid vacation per month.

* 3 hours of paid sick leave per month.

* Miles at 57 cents per mile.

Responsibilities:

Duties would include (but would not be limited to): 

* Screening and assessing for in Home and Supervised visit services and needs. 

* Assists families coming into program to create goals which will enable them to obtain and sustain self-sufficiency. 

* Provide case management and support services to children and their families, to promote stabilization and safety. 

* Provide concrete services in the home as needed to include, development of parenting skills, organization, structure, activities, and resource utilization. 

* Conduct supervised visits and create a working relationship between caretakers and family members establishing appropriate limit setting and follow through. 

* Participate in family meetings and courts. 

* Attend meetings as specified by the supervisor and discuss progress to determine possible changes or other adjustments to schedules and treatment goals and objectives. 

* Work a flexible schedule that may include evenings and weekends based on 40 hours per week. 

* Staff may be required to use their own vehicle for transportation of clients.

Qualifications:

* Master's Degree in Social Work, Psychology or related human services field preferred. 

* Bachelor degree in Social Work, Psychology or Human Services Field with one year of relevant experience and/or training in mentioned responsibilities required. 

* Bilingual (English-Spanish) required. 

* Professional appearance. 

* Excellent communication skills both verbal and written. 

* Knowledge of resources in Denver Metro area. 

* Ability to effectively work individually and in a team environment. 

* Ability to meet employer's responsibilities, policies and procedures. 

* Willingness to submit to a pre-employment drug screening, motor vehicle record check, trails and criminal background check.

* Screening and assessing for in Home and Supervised visit services and needs.
